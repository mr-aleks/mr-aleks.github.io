Counter-Strike: Global Offensive

"Map Callouts" are used to communicate with your team where you are, where the enemy is, and everything in between.

Please keep in mind that these call-outs will vary by region, team, and version of CS. It's OK to have different call-outs from what you see here. These are not some "standard", they are just very common. Hopefully people use them universally so we can all communicate better in matchmaking.
